Who We Are

Safyrus is a prominent creative design studio founded on the passion and expertise of a group of freelancers.

Our adventure began in the dynamic city of Hyderabad in 2018, when we set out to redefine the boundaries of design. Recognising the great potential of the Himalayan foothills, we broadened our horizons and built another office in Rishikesh in 2019, gaining inspiration from the stunning surroundings. However, the unusual events of 2020 compelled us to adjust and accept a remote work model, as did many others.

As we head towards 2023, we continue to prosper and create excellent creative solutions from the convenience of our home offices, fuelled by our unrelenting passion and innovative attitude. Join us on this incredible voyage.
